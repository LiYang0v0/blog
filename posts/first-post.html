Posted on 二零二六年 七月三日July 3, 2026

如果LLM对「学术」的影响，至以其完全成为「术」，则「学」之体验完全被LLM所「遮蔽」。抑或这一「魔力的」文字生成及推理工具彻底俘获了学者，则「成为」学者即愈发接近「成为」模板中的吐字机器，那么真正的「沉思」将以更快的速度消失。

If the influence of LLMs on 'episteme' (ἐπιστήμη) reaches the point where it completely reduces 'episteme' to mere 'techne' (τέχνη), then the experience of 'episteme' becomes entirely obscured by LLMs. Or, if this 'magical' tool for text generation and reasoning thoroughly captures scholars, then 'becoming' a scholar increasingly means 'becoming' a template-bound word-spewing machine, in which case genuine contemplation will vanish at an even faster rate.
